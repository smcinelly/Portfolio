Food Candle Digest Email Newsletter

Food Candle Digest is a static frontend Email Newsletter written in HTML.

Overview:
This project was a team approach as an introduction to HTML email newsletters. It was coded in CodePen.io. We chose the topic of food scented candles because we wanted to bring humor into the project as well as high contrasting colors.

My role in the project was that of designer and developer.
