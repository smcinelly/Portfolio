About Stephanie!

Stephanie McInelly is a junior front-end web developer. Stephanie is interested in using her understanding of human connection to create meaningful work.

Stephanie is skilled in working remotely with a team. Using technical tools, Stephanie communicates well to both clients and teammates. She thrives in fast-paced working environments and efficiently works on multiple projects simultaneously. Stephanie would like to work with bright people who are passionate about their work, supports learning and growth, and knows how to have fun.

Currently, Stephanie is a student at The Moms Can: Code School, working to build her coding skills and set out into the tech field.
